        • Cross training or casual wear

          LL - 16 Apr 2024

          These shoes def have an updated “dad” shoe vibe! I love that they’re made using recycled materials. The metallic detail looks good in contrast with the mesh parts of the shoe. I wear these shoes for cross training workouts (think treadmill and floor exercises), because they aren’t overly supportive. I have trouble finding good cross training shoes, because I want something supportive enough to run in but not something that is going to interfere with exercises on the floor like squats or lunges. These shoes are extremely lightweight without feeling cheap. Honestly one of my favorite things is that they have very good traction! I also use these shoes for a day with a lot of walking. They would totally work for a casual look, too. Just beware that like a lot of Nike’s, they’re pretty narrow. I don’t have wide feet, so they work well for me… I don’t know how comfortable they would be if you wore super thick socks. The tongue doesn’t move around a lot, and the laces are puffy and short. I see how they could be hard for someone to tie. While I like black shoes for working out, I think the other color ways would look very trendy for street wear!...
